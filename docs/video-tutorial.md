# PayStream Video Tutorial — Stream Creation

This video tutorial walks through a complete PayStream stream lifecycle in under 10 minutes.

## What the tutorial covers

- Wallet setup and connecting a Stellar wallet
- Creating a stream from employer to employee
- Withdrawing earned tokens from the stream

## Why watch it

This end-to-end screencast is ideal for new users who want to see the full `create_stream → withdraw` flow in action.

- Includes captions/subtitles for accessibility
- Focused on the core workflow for stream setup and withdrawal
- Uses the PayStream demo and CLI flows where relevant
